# The Vigar Line — family tree

The public presentation of a genealogical research project tracing the ancestry
of **Sarah Vigar** up both sides — the paternal Vigar / Prince / Biddlecombe /
Wyburn / Ashton lines (the Somerset Levels and London to the Manitoba prairie)
and the maternal McDonald / McDougall / Vogan / Smith / Woods / Rafferty / Taylor
lines (Ontario and Ulster to the British Columbia coast).

**[View the tree →](https://jitousig.github.io/sarah-family-tree-site/)**

Every claim is graded — **Sourced** (primary record or firm correlation),
**Reported** (secondary or testimony), or **To prove** — and cited to the
parish, date, and archival reference behind it.

## Privacy & sources

This page embeds **no record images**: the census, parish, and marriage scans
that underpin the research are held privately and are never republished, in
keeping with their licensing. Only the citations — a parish, a date, an archival
reference such as `GRO 5c/582` — appear here, and those are facts, freely shared.
The living generation is shown by name only, with no private detail.
